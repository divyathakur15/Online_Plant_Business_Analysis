## 🔍 Chart Types Explained

**Box & Whisker:** Shows min, Q1, median, Q3, max, outliers
**Histogram:** Shows frequency across ranges
**Bar Chart:** Horizontal comparison of categories
**Column Chart:** Vertical comparison of categories
**Pie Chart:** Percentage breakdown (parts of whole)
**Donut Chart:** Similar to pie, with center hole

## ✨ Professional Naming Convention

Format: [Number]_[Category]_[Metric]_[ChartType].PNG

Benefits:
✓ Sequential ordering
✓ Self-descriptive
✓ Chart type visible
✓ Easy searching
✓ Professional appearance
